About Us

Hunt for previous year question papers during the time of examination is a common habit among students. They have to spend most of their time and efforts in searching for this. E-helper is formed as a solution for this problem by the students of Nirmala College Muvattupuzha. This offers question papers for most of the UG and PG courses under MG UNiversity. This is a non-profit initative for the well-being of all students. Site will be update from time to time.
